What is Particulate Matter 2.5?

➔ Mixture of solid particles & liquid droplets in the air

➔ Correlated with respiratory problems & mortality

➔ Requires expensive ground monitors to accurately measure areas for particulate matter

Natural Causes

  • Forest Fires
  • Dust Storms
  • Volcanic Eruptions

Man-Made Causes

  • Automobiles
  • Power Plants
  • Burning Fossil Fuels

How Can This Problem Be Addressed Economically?

    ➔ Create a model using supervised learning to predict particulate matter and remidiate at located hotspots

    ➔ Find a more readily-available solution that can be freely used worldwide (satellite imagery)

    ➔ Train model using accurate, recorded ground monitor data across the continental United States

    ➔ Apply this model to other countries and time periods
